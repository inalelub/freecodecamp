Standard Upright Tombstone

R500 - R2000

This is the most common type of tombstone, and it is a simple, upright slab of stone with the deceased's name, dates, and a brief inscription.

Custom Upright Tombstone

R2000 - R5000

A custom upright tombstone is similar to a standard upright tombstone, but it has more customization options, such as different shapes, sizes, and materials.

Flat Marker Tombstone

R1000 - R3000

A flat marker tombstone is a low-lying slab of stone that is placed directly on the ground. It is a more affordable option than an upright tombstone, and it can be customized with different shapes, sizes, and materials.
